Raining Embers

 

Raining Embers is a casual friendly heroic raiding guild. We raid on Mondays and Wednesdays from 9:00 PM - 11:00 PM EST for main raid, and Saturdays from 8:00 PM - 11:00 PM EST for alt raid. We help our members become better players and prefer coaching over culling. We also dabble in a multitude of content such as Mythic Plus groups, Housing Contests, PvP, and more. 

We are a friendly all inclusive guild, We pride on making sure everyone plays the game how they want to pay for it as you are the one paying we will not tell you how to play, Wanna play the off meta, No worries. Hit us up today Large guild 4 neighbourhoods. Started Guild 09/23/2025 . Always looking for people to join our little family!
